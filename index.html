    How does Tradie help users find the best entry points in the market

    Regardless of whether the market is sideways, down, or in an uptrend, there is often a repeating pattern where certain coins are chosen for pump and dump schemes every few weeks/months. By the time we become aware of it, it's usually at its peak. All pump and dump cases tend to follow these common patterns:

    • - Prior to the pump, there is a long period of sideways or downtrend with minimal price and volume fluctuations.
    • - When the pump starts, the volume/price suddenly surges within a short period (accompanied by potentially positive news).
    • - The price movement becomes independent of BTC.

    With appropriate settings, traders can monitor these signals anytime, anywhere, staying ahead of trends as quickly as possible.
